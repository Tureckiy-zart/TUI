# Retention Policy

**Date Created:** 2025-12-25  
**Status:** ✅ **ACTIVE**  
**Purpose:** Define rules for retaining governance review reports and historical documentation

---

## Policy Statement

Governance review reports and historical documentation must be kept indefinitely for historical comparison and future reference.

---

## Retention Rules

### Keep All Review Reports Indefinitely

**Requirement:** All governance review reports must be kept:

- ✅ Keep all review reports indefinitely
- ✅ Do NOT delete review reports
- ✅ Preserve review reports for historical context
- ✅ Maintain review reports in accessible location

**Rationale:** Review reports provide historical context and enable comparison across review cycles.

### Use for Historical Comparison

**Requirement:** Review reports should be used for comparison:

- ✅ Compare current state with previous reviews
- ✅ Track progress across review cycles
- ✅ Identify trends and patterns
- ✅ Learn from historical decisions

**Usage:**
- Reference previous reviews when conducting new reviews
- Compare metrics and findings across cycles
- Track improvement over time

### Reference in Future Reviews

**Requirement:** Review reports should be referenced in future reviews:

- ✅ Reference previous reviews in new reviews
- ✅ Build on previous findings
- ✅ Maintain continuity across review cycles
- ✅ Document evolution of governance process

**Format:**
- Include references to previous reviews in new review documents
- Link to previous review reports
- Compare findings and outcomes

---

## Retention Scope

### Governance Review Reports

**Included:**
- ✅ Governance review reports (`GOVERNANCE_REVIEW_YYYY-MM-DD.md`)
- ✅ Review templates and checklists
- ✅ Review findings and recommendations
- ✅ Review outcomes and action items

**Location:**
- `docs/governance/reviews/` - Review reports
- `docs/governance/` - Review templates and cycle documentation

### Historical Documentation

**Included:**
- ✅ Audit reports
- ✅ Foundation lock reports
- ✅ Migration documentation
- ✅ Historical context documents

**Location:**
- `docs/reports/` - Project reports
- `docs/reports/audit/` - Audit reports
- `docs/migrations/` - Migration documentation

---

## Archive Naming Convention

### Review Report Naming

**Format:** `GOVERNANCE_REVIEW_YYYY-MM-DD.md`

**Examples:**
- `GOVERNANCE_REVIEW_2025-12-19.md`
- `GOVERNANCE_REVIEW_INITIAL.md`

**Rules:**
- ✅ Use ISO date format (YYYY-MM-DD)
- ✅ Use descriptive names for special reviews (e.g., `INITIAL`)
- ✅ Maintain consistent naming convention

### Historical Context

- Understand evolution of governance process
- Track decisions and rationale over time

**Benefit:** Retention enables comparison:

- Compare metrics across review cycles

- Track improvement over time
